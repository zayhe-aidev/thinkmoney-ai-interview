# thinkmoney Fee Schedule

## Monthly Account Fees

| Account Type | Monthly Fee |
|---|---|
| Standard | Free |
| Premium | £9.99 |
| Business | £4.99 |

## ATM Withdrawal Fees

| Location | Standard Account | Premium Account |
|---|---|---|
| UK ATMs | Free (up to 10/month, then £0.50 each) | Free (unlimited) |
| EEA ATMs | £1.50 per withdrawal | First 5 free/month, then £1.50 |
| Rest of World ATMs | 2% of amount (min £1.50) | First 5 free/month, then 2% (min £1.50) |

Daily ATM withdrawal limits: Standard £300, Premium £500, Business £1,000.

## Currency Exchange Fees

| Scenario | Standard Account | Premium Account |
|---|---|---|
| Weekday (Mon-Fri) | 0.5% markup on interbank rate | Free up to £1,000/month, then 0.5% |
| Weekend (Sat-Sun) & Bank Holidays | 1.5% markup on interbank rate | 1.0% markup on interbank rate |

Weekend rates are higher because the foreign exchange markets are closed and we apply a buffer to protect against Monday opening volatility.
