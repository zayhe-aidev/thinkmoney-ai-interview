# thinkmoney Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I open a thinkmoney account?

To open a thinkmoney account, you need to:
1. Be a UK resident aged 18 or over.
2. Download the thinkmoney app or visit our website.
3. Provide your full name, date of birth, and UK address.
4. Complete identity verification by uploading a valid photo ID (passport or driving licence) and a selfie.
5. Your account is typically approved within 24 hours, though some applications may take up to 3 business days.

You will receive a virtual card immediately upon approval. A physical card will be posted to your registered address within 5-7 business days.

## How do I reset my PIN or password?

**App password/biometric reset:**
- Open the thinkmoney app and tap "Forgotten password?"
- You will receive a verification code via SMS to your registered mobile number.
- Follow the prompts to set a new password.

**Card PIN reset:**
- Log into the thinkmoney app.
- Go to Cards > Select your card > Reset PIN.
- Your new PIN will be sent via push notification (or SMS if push is unavailable).
- You must use Chip & PIN at an ATM or terminal before contactless will work again.

## What currencies does thinkmoney support?

thinkmoney accounts support the following currencies:
- **GBP** (British Pound) — primary account currency
- **EUR** (Euro)
- **USD** (US Dollar)
- **PLN** (Polish Zloty)
- **RON** (Romanian Leu)

You can hold balances in all supported currencies simultaneously. Currency exchange is available 24/7 in the app, though weekend rates include a markup (see our fees schedule).

## Is my money protected?

thinkmoney is an authorised Electronic Money Institution (EMI) regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA). Your funds are safeguarded in segregated accounts held with tier-1 UK banks in accordance with the Electronic Money Regulations 2011.

**Important:** E-money is not covered by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS). However, safeguarding means your funds are ring-fenced and protected even if thinkmoney were to become insolvent.

## How do I close my thinkmoney account?

To close your account:
1. Ensure your balance is zero by transferring all funds to another account.
2. Cancel any active standing orders or direct debits.
3. Contact us via in-app chat or phone and request account closure.
4. We will confirm closure within 5 business days.

Note: We are required to retain your transaction data for 5 years after closure for regulatory purposes.

## What happens if my account is restricted?

Account restrictions may be applied for:
- **KYC requirements:** We may need additional documents to verify your identity.
- **Suspicious activity:** Unusual transaction patterns may trigger a temporary hold.
- **Regulatory compliance:** We may be legally required to restrict your account.

If your account is restricted, you will receive a notification explaining what action is needed. Most restrictions are lifted within 24-48 hours once the required information is provided.
