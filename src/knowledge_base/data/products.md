# thinkmoney Products & Services

## thinkmoney Standard Account

Our free everyday account with all the essentials:

- **Monthly fee:** Free
- **Debit card:** Visa debit (physical + virtual)
- **Contactless payments:** Up to £100 per tap
- **ATM withdrawals:** Up to £300 per day
- **Daily spending limit:** £5,000
- **Monthly spending limit:** £20,000
- **Supported currencies:** GBP, EUR, USD
- **Instant bank transfers:** Free (Faster Payments)
- **Direct debits & standing orders:** Supported
- **Budgeting tools:** Basic spending categories and notifications
- **In-app chat support:** 24/7

## thinkmoney Premium Account

Everything in Standard, plus enhanced limits and perks:

- **Monthly fee:** £9.99/month
- **Debit card:** Metal Visa debit card (physical) + unlimited virtual cards
- **ATM withdrawals:** Up to £500 per day, first 5 international ATM withdrawals free per month
- **Daily spending limit:** £15,000
- **Monthly spending limit:** £50,000
- **Supported currencies:** GBP, EUR, USD, PLN, RON
- **Fee-free currency exchange:** Up to £1,000/month at interbank rate (weekdays)
- **Travel insurance:** Worldwide travel insurance for trips up to 45 days
- **Purchase protection:** Up to £1,000 per claim for items bought with your thinkmoney card
- **Priority support:** Dedicated phone line, average wait time under 2 minutes
- **Advanced budgeting:** Savings pots, round-ups, salary sorter

## thinkmoney Business Account

For sole traders and small businesses:

- **Monthly fee:** £4.99/month
- **Invoicing tools:** Create and send invoices from the app
- **Expense categorisation:** Automatic tax-category tagging
- **Multi-user access:** Add employees with customisable spending controls
- **HMRC integration:** Export transaction data in HMRC-compatible format
- **Business debit cards:** Up to 5 cards per account
- **Daily spending limit:** £25,000
- **Accounting software integration:** Xero, QuickBooks, FreeAgent

## Savings Pots

Flexible savings within your thinkmoney account:

- **Instant access pots:** Create up to 10 savings pots with custom names and goals.
- **Round-ups:** Automatically round up card transactions to the nearest £1 and save the difference.
- **Scheduled savings:** Set up automatic transfers to pots on payday or any schedule.
- **Interest rate:** 3.5% AER on balances up to £10,000 (variable, Premium accounts only).
- **Withdrawals:** Instant transfer back to your main balance, no penalties.

## International Transfers

Send money abroad quickly and transparently:

- **SEPA transfers (EUR):** Free, arrives within 1 business day.
- **Faster Payments (GBP domestic):** Free, arrives within minutes.
- **SWIFT transfers (international):** £3.00 fee, arrives within 2-5 business days depending on the destination.
- **Exchange rates:** Real-time interbank rate plus a small markup (see fees schedule).
- **Transfer limits:** Up to £25,000 per single transfer. Higher amounts require enhanced verification.
- **Supported destination countries:** Over 40 countries. See the app for the full list.
- **Tracking:** Real-time transfer status tracking in the app.
